Courses & Learning

We offer courses in Web Development, Data Science, Mobile Development, UI/UX Design, and Cybersecurity. Each course is designed to be practical and job-ready, with real projects you can add to your portfolio.

Not at all. We have beginner, intermediate, and advanced courses. Our beginner courses assume zero prior knowledge — just bring a laptop and the willingness to learn.

We offer both options. Our in-person classes are held at our Westlands campus in Nairobi. Online classes are live and interactive via Zoom, with recordings available for 30 days.

Course lengths vary. Beginner courses run 8–12 weeks, intermediate courses 10–14 weeks, and advanced courses up to 16 weeks. All include weekend sessions so working professionals can attend.

Payment & Fees

Certification

Yes. Every student who completes a course and passes the final project receives a TechLearn Kenya certificate. Certificates are issued both digitally and in print, and can be verified online by employers.

Our certificates are recognized by over 40 partner companies including Safaricom, Andela, Cellulant, and several Nairobi startups. We also prepare students for globally recognized certifications like AWS, Google, and Microsoft exams.
