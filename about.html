Our Mission

TechLearn Kenya was founded in 2021 with a single goal: to make world-class tech education accessible to every Kenyan, regardless of their background or financial situation.

We partner with industry professionals and universities to deliver practical, job-ready courses in web development, data science, cybersecurity, and mobile development — all taught in a way that fits the Kenyan context.
